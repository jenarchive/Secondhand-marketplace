## Project overview

GreenCheck AI is an innovative software prototype focused on Explainable AI (XAI) for sustainability fraud (greenwashing) 
detection. This project directly addresses the "black box" problem of traditional AI by providing clear, plain-language explanations for every flagged sustainability claim in corporate reports and public data.

## Client Information

### Client: Legal Pythia

Legal Pythia is a legal tech startup that strives to help legal, finance and government organisations to manage large volumes of documents and data more efficiently using Explainable Artificial Intelligence (XAI). Their tools aim to allow clients to increase the efficiency of identifying fraud, detecting greenwashing and managing administrative/legal workflows.
